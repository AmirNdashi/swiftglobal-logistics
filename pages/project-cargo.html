About Our Project Cargo Service

Project cargo requires expertise, meticulous planning, and coordination across multiple modes of transport. SwiftGlobal's dedicated project logistics team has successfully delivered some of the most complex and challenging cargo movements — from industrial turbines and power plant equipment to offshore structures and large-scale construction machinery.

Every project cargo movement begins with a detailed feasibility study and route survey, ensuring every challenge is identified and solved before a single piece of cargo moves.

Project Cargo Solutions

Heavy-Lift Operations

Specialized cranes and heavy-lift vessels for cargo exceeding standard weight and dimension limits.

Route Surveys & Permits

Detailed route planning, engineering surveys, and permit acquisition for oversized road transport.

Break-Bulk Shipping

Non-containerized ocean freight for cargo too large or awkward for standard containers.

Multi-Modal Projects

Seamlessly combining sea, air, and road transport to move complex cargo across any distance.

Key Benefits

Dedicated Project Manager

Every project cargo shipment gets a dedicated manager who oversees every detail from origin to delivery.

Engineering Expertise

Our in-house engineers assess load plans, lifting requirements, and structural constraints for safe transport.

Global Partner Network

We work with specialized heavy-lift carriers, port operators, and local agents worldwide for seamless execution.

Full Insurance Coverage

Comprehensive cargo insurance for all project cargo movements, including heavy-lift and high-value shipments.

How It Works

01

Initial Consultation

Share your cargo specifications — dimensions, weight, origin, destination, and timeline. We assess feasibility.

02

Route Survey & Planning

Our team conducts a full route survey, identifying any obstacles, permits required, and optimal transport methods.

03

Custom Solution Design

We design a bespoke multi-modal transport plan with full cost breakdown and risk assessment.

04

Coordinated Execution

Our project manager coordinates all carriers, ports, cranes, and border agents for seamless execution.

05

Safe Delivery & Reporting

Cargo delivered safely on schedule. Full documentation, photos, and delivery report provided on completion.
