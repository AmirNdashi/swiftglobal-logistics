About Our Land Freight Service

SwiftGlobal's road freight network covers local, national, and cross-border routes with a modern GPS-tracked fleet and a network of trusted carrier partners. We offer flexible scheduling, door-to-door delivery, and complete transparency at every mile.

Whether you're moving regular pallets or oversized industrial equipment, our land freight specialists design the right routing and vehicle type for your cargo's specific requirements.

Land Freight Options

FTL — Full Truckload

Dedicated truck for your cargo only. Fastest road option with direct point-to-point routing and maximum security.

LTL — Less than Truckload

Share truck space with other shippers and pay only for the space you use. Cost-effective for smaller loads.

Refrigerated Transport

Temperature-controlled trucks for food, pharmaceuticals, and other perishable goods requiring cold chain integrity.

Cross-Border Transport

Expert cross-border routing with all customs handling included. Seamless transit across international checkpoints.

Key Benefits

GPS-Tracked Fleet

Every vehicle in our network is GPS-tracked so you always know exactly where your cargo is on the road.

Flexible Scheduling

We work around your schedule — same-day, next-day, and planned long-haul services available.

Cross-Border Expertise

Our drivers and border agents have years of experience clearing international checkpoints smoothly and quickly.

Door-to-Door Delivery

We collect from the sender's location and deliver directly to the recipient — no middlemen, no delays.
