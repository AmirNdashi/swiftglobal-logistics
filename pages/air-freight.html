About Our Air Freight Service

When your cargo cannot wait, SwiftGlobal's air freight service provides the fastest connection between your supplier and your customer. We partner with leading airlines worldwide to offer both express and economy air cargo options tailored to your timeline and budget.

From single urgent packages to full charter flights for large-volume shipments, our air freight specialists handle every detail — documentation, customs, airport handling, and last-mile delivery.

Air Freight Options

Express Air Freight

Next-day or 2-day delivery for the most urgent shipments. Priority handling and guaranteed space.

Standard Air Freight

Cost-effective scheduled air services with reliable transit times for non-urgent international cargo.

Charter Flights

Full aircraft charters for oversized, high-volume, or extremely time-critical cargo requirements.

Perishables & Pharma

GDP-compliant cold chain air freight for pharmaceuticals, fresh produce, and biologics.

Key Benefits

Global Airline Network

Access to 200+ airline partners including Emirates SkyCargo, Lufthansa Cargo, Qatar Airways Cargo, and more.

Door-to-Door Service

We collect from your door and deliver to the recipient — handling every step including customs clearance.

Real-Time Flight Tracking

Monitor your air shipment live from departure to arrival with our universal tracking platform.

Dangerous Goods Certified

IATA-certified handling of dangerous goods including lithium batteries, chemicals, and aerosols.
