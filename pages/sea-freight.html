About Our Sea Freight Service

SwiftGlobal Logistics offers comprehensive ocean freight solutions for businesses of every size. Whether you're shipping a single pallet or filling multiple containers, our experienced team manages every step of the process — from booking and documentation to port handling and final delivery.

We work with the world's leading shipping lines to secure competitive rates and reliable schedules on all major trade lanes, including Asia–Africa, Europe–Africa, and transatlantic routes.

Shipping Options

FCL — Full Container Load

Ideal for large shipments. You get exclusive use of a 20ft or 40ft container for maximum security and efficiency.

LCL — Less than Container Load

Share container space with other shippers. Perfect for smaller cargo volumes with the same global reach.

RoRo — Roll-on/Roll-off

Specialized shipping for vehicles, machinery, and wheeled cargo. Drive-on, sail, drive-off — simple and safe.

Reefer Containers

Temperature-controlled shipping for perishables, pharmaceuticals, and other climate-sensitive cargo.

Key Benefits

Competitive Ocean Rates

Access to preferential rates from all major carriers including MSC, Maersk, CMA CGM, and COSCO.

End-to-End Visibility

Real-time vessel tracking and proactive shipment updates at every stage of the journey.

Full Documentation Support

Our team handles all shipping documents including Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin, and packing lists.

Hazardous Cargo Certified

We are certified to handle and ship dangerous goods (IMO/IMDG compliant) safely and legally.
