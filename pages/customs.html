About Our Customs Clearance Service

Navigating customs regulations can be complex, time-consuming, and costly if not handled correctly. SwiftGlobal's licensed customs brokers take the complexity out of international trade — managing every document, declaration, duty, and inspection on your behalf.

We stay current with all regulatory updates across every market we operate in, so you can move goods across borders with full confidence and zero surprises.

Customs Services

Import Clearance

Full handling of all import formalities — tariff classification, duty payment, inspection liaison, and release.

Export Clearance

Export declarations, licensing, certificate of origin, and all paperwork required for international departures.

Duty & Tax Advisory

Accurate duty and tax calculation to help you plan your costs and avoid unexpected charges at the border.

Compliance Consulting

Trade compliance audits and advisory to ensure your import/export operations meet all regulatory requirements.

Key Benefits

Licensed & Experienced Brokers

Our customs team holds all required licenses and has decades of combined experience across multiple jurisdictions.

Fast Clearance Times

We prioritize speed without compromising compliance — most shipments cleared within hours of arrival.

Duty Optimization

We identify applicable trade agreements, exemptions, and preferential tariff rates to minimize your duty costs legally.

Full Documentation Management

From commercial invoices to phytosanitary certificates — we prepare, review, and submit all required documents.

How It Works

01

Document Collection

You provide us with your commercial invoice, packing list, and Bill of Lading or Air Waybill.

02

Classification & Assessment

We classify your goods under the correct HS tariff code and calculate applicable duties and taxes.

03

Declaration Filing

We file the customs declaration electronically with the relevant customs authority on your behalf.

04

Inspection Handling

If selected for inspection, we coordinate with customs officers and provide all necessary documentation.

05

Release & Delivery

Once cleared, we arrange immediate release and coordinate onward transport to your final destination.
