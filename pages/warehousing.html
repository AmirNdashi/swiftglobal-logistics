About Our Warehousing Service

SwiftGlobal operates modern, fully secured warehouse facilities strategically located for efficient distribution. Our warehouses are equipped with advanced inventory management systems, 24/7 CCTV surveillance, and expert staff to ensure your goods are stored safely and managed with precision.

Whether you need short-term storage during transit or long-term warehousing for distribution, we offer flexible terms and complete visibility into your stock at all times.

Warehousing Options

Ambient Storage

Standard storage for general goods at ambient temperature. Racking systems optimized for easy access and space efficiency.

Cold Storage

Refrigerated and frozen storage for perishables, food products, and temperature-sensitive pharmaceuticals.

High-Security Storage

Enhanced security vaults for high-value goods, electronics, jewelry, and confidential cargo.

Pick, Pack & Fulfillment

End-to-end order fulfillment services — receive, store, pick, pack, and ship to your customers.

Key Benefits

24/7 CCTV & Security

Round-the-clock surveillance, access control, and on-site security personnel protect your goods at all times.

Live Inventory Dashboard

Access your stock levels, inbound/outbound activity, and reports in real time via our client portal.

Flexible Terms

Short-term, long-term, and seasonal storage options available. Only pay for the space you actually use.

Fully Insured Facility

All goods stored with SwiftGlobal are covered by comprehensive warehouse insurance for your peace of mind.

How It Works

01

Discuss Your Needs

Tell us your cargo type, volume, and storage duration. We'll recommend the right storage solution.

02

Space Allocated

We allocate dedicated storage space and configure racking or shelving to fit your cargo specifications.

03

Goods Received

Your cargo arrives at our facility. Our team verifies, photographs, and logs each item into our system.

04

Storage & Management

Goods are stored safely. You monitor stock levels and request dispatches anytime via our client portal.

05

Dispatch on Request

When you're ready, we pick, pack, and dispatch your goods to any destination worldwide.
