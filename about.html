Our Story

From Local Carrier to Global Logistics Partner

SwiftGlobal Logistics was founded in 2009 with a single mission: to make global freight simple, reliable, and transparent for every business — no matter its size.

What began as a small regional freight operation has grown into a full-service logistics company serving clients across more than 120 countries. Our growth has been built on one foundation — the trust of our clients.

Today, SwiftGlobal Logistics handles thousands of shipments every month across sea, air, and land — backed by a team of logistics experts, a state-of-the-art tracking platform, and an unwavering commitment to on-time delivery.
